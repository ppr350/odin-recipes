Yakisoba

Description

Making a decent Yakisoba (Japanese fried noodles) is fairly simple. All it takes just 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • yakisoba sauce
  • lettuce
  • carrot
  • noodles
  • prawns
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat up some oil in a fry pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Get your noodles ready following instructions on its packaging.
  3. Cook and stir carrot, lettuce in hot oil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add noodles to the fry pan, spread it, and try not to stir it too much.
  5. add yakisoba sauce, then add some hot water.
  6. Now stir the sauce into the mixture.
  7. The dish is now ready!
