Ramen

Description

I am not a good cook myself, but I do like Ramen. A properly prepared ramen broth is too time-consuming for my lazy ass. So I just make up a simplfied slow cooker chicken soup ramen.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 Chicken leg
  • 4 pieces seaweed
  • 2 small nori sheets
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 4 teaspoon mirin
  • 100g spring onions
  • 1x egg
  • ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Put chicken leg, seaweed, mirin, chicken stock, MSG, spring onions into slow cooker.
  2. Add 1.7L water.
  3. Cover it with lid and let it cook for at least 8 hours.
  4. Boil egg in hot water for 10 minutes, then refrigerate it.
  5. Get your noodles ready following instructions on its packaging.
  6. Pour chicken soup to bowl
  7. Cut boiled egg in half
  8. Add noodles, egg, chopped spring oinions and nori sheets into soup bowl, and it's ready to serve!
