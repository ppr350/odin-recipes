Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a wellknown Japanese dish that is relatively easy to make with little ingredients.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 4 pieces seaweed
  • bunito flakes
  • miso paste
  • 2 x carrot

Steps

  1. Put bunito flakes and seaweed into a saucepan, add hot water.
  2. cover it with lid, and cook it over low to medium heat for 30 minutes.
  3. Filter the bonito flakes and seaweed out.
  4. Now we have dashi water.
  5. chopped carrot to cube shape, put it into dashi water and cook it over high heat.
  6. Once it starts boiling, turn off the heat source.
  7. Put miso paste to dashi water, ready to serve!
